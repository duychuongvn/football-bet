Crypto P2P Betting

Bether is one of the first peer-to-peer systems developed on the Ethereum blockchain. Bettors can create a new bet for any open matches or settle an existing one which was placed by others.

All matches will be managed by smart contacts deployed on the Ethereum blockchain. After the match is decided, the payout will be sent out to the people who betted correctly. Other than betting on the wrong team, there is no way of losing Ether. The design of the deployed Bet smart contracts makes this betting dApp (distributed application) fraud- and tamper-proof! Your bets are safely stored in the smart contracts. In case of worst scenarios (match is postponed, external data provider misbehaves, etc), you are always able to get all your Ether back without paying any fee!

The design of the smart contracts representing the various bets makes betting on Crypto P2P 100% fraud-proof . There are two outcomes of a bet:

1, The match is finished and the result is first correctly fetched and the confirmed. Subsequently, users get their payouts.

2, Something goes wrong, e.g. the match is postponed, the fetching does not return any match results or the result is not confirmed in time. In any of these cases, users get their bets fully refunded without paying any fee!
