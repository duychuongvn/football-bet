Bether is one of the first peer-to-peer betting systems developed on the Ethereum blockchain. Bettors can create a new bet for any open matches or settle an existing one which was placed by others.

All matches will be managed by smart contacts deployed on the Ethereum blockchain. After the match is decided, the payouts will be sent out to the bettors who betted correctly. Other than betting on the wrong team, there is no way of losing Ether. The design of the deployed Bet smart contracts makes this betting dApp (distributed application) fraud- and tamper-proof! Your bets are safely stored in the smart contracts. In case of worst scenarios (match is postponed, external data provider misbehaves, etc), you are always able to get all your Ether back without paying any fee!
