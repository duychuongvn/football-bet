What do I need to start betting?

A computer running Chrome or FireFox

1. Can I bet on a mobile device ?

- For now, we don’t recommend betting on mobile devices.

2. Can I login from multiple computers ?

- You can use Bether from multiple computers as long as you have MetaMask installed on both. When you log in to MetaMask on a new computer, it will request your “seed phrase” for verification. Copy your MetaMask seed phrase from the old computer to the new computer to ensure you have the same secure keys.

3. Can I use a different browser ?

- We recommend sticking with Chrome or Firefox since we can’t guarantee optimal performance.
A digital wallet

1. Install your digital wallet

- To bet via Bether, you need a digital wallet. We recommend to use MetaMask since it is simple, easy to use and guarantees high security.

- You’ll need to put ether in your wallet to make your first bet, please note that a digital wallet like MetaMask acts like a bank account, therefore make sure you don’t forget your password or the seed words.

2. Reinstalling MetaMask

- Some users need to uninstall and reinstall MetaMask because the app needs a refresh. As long as you kept your seed words safely, this is very easy! Just delete the extension, reinstall it, and import your twelve seed words. Then you’ll set the password you want to use (this can be the same one as you used before or a whole new password).

3. What are seed words? Why are they so important?

- Your seed words are like a very important password. They let you restore your MetaMask account in the case it’s deleted. If MetaMask is uninstalled from your computer, or if you need to use a different computer, you can use your seed words to reestablish access to your wallet. This also means that anyone with your seed words can access your account and steal your assets. Please be sure to save your seed words, and do not share them with others.

4. Why is MetaMask Locked?

- Occasionally the “My bets” page displays a lock screen. This happens because MetaMask automatically locks your account after a certain period of time. To unlock, simply click on the MetaMask extension and type in your password.

5. Can you change my MetaMask password?

- No, we can’t.

5. What is a “wallet address”?

- Your public wallet address (e.g. 0x00a7d97ed55406fb27590c093dc08a73092a55b9) is a unique identifier for your wallet. You can share it freely with others and it’s used to direct assets to your wallet.
Ethereum, a digital currency

1. What’s ether (ETH)? Why do I need it?

- Ether is a digital currency that powers the Ethereum network, which is what Bether is built on. Ether acts like any other currency - its value fluctuates with the market.

- You need to buy or transfer ether from other sources to Metamask wallet to create or settle your bets.

2. How do I get ether?

- For U.S. citizens only: you can buy ether (ETH) directly through MetaMask. MetaMask purchases ether through Coinbase (a currency exchange), and delivers it to your MetaMask wallet.

For everyone else: you will need to purchase ETH from an exchange, and then transfer the ETH from your exchange wallet to your MetaMask wallet.

3. How do I send ether to MetaMask?

- You can copy your MetaMask wallet address by clicking the three large dots next to your username in MetaMask and selecting “Copy address to clipboard”. You can send assets to your MetaMask wallet from another source by inputting this wallet address.

4. How long does it take to receive ether?

- Basing on the current traffic of Ethereum network, it usually takes under 10 minutes to receive ether in your MetaMask wallet upon transferring.

Create and settle bets

How do I know my new bet created successfully?

You will be noticed by a pop-up telling your bet is created successfully and from there you can share via social media. Your bet will be displayed in match details with your wallet address and in “My bets” as well.

Can I adjust the odds or stake after my new bets are created?

No, you can’t. However, if your bet is still under the open status, you can cancel it and recreate again.
Can I cancel an open bet?

Yes, you can. As long as your bet status is open, you can cancel it. Once it is changed to settled, you can’t cancel.

How do I know when my bets are settled?

You can check all your bets in “My bets” and filter by Settled. Alternatively, you can access directly Match Details page and search by your wallet address.
Can I cancel a settled bet?

No, you can’t.
How much time should I wait for to receive the payouts for a winning or draw bet?

Once the match result is confirmed by the result API and our Admin (two factors confirmation), your payouts will be sent out.
How can I be prioritized to receive the payouts before others?

After the match was finished and you won the bet, you can click on “Request for Payout” under Finished Matches to prioritize your payouts. There are no any other fees for prioritizing your payouts except the transfer fee (Gas) that you incur for miners.

Transactions and Technical Questions

What’s Etherscan?

Etherscan is a read-only interface for the Ethereum network. It tells you everything that happened on the blockchain. It helps you verify that transactions were successful, and tracks other helpful details about transactions and addresses.
Why did my transaction fail?

If your transaction failed, it was probably because someone else settled the bet that you are choosing. Another possible reason a transaction might fail is manually setting the gas limit too low.
My transaction failed but it still charged me gas.

Gas is paid whenever a request is processed on the blockchain. Even if a transaction fails, it still takes computing power to determine whether or not the transaction can succeed. The Ethereum network charges gas for the operations executed up until the transaction fails. These transaction fees are not paid to the Bether team, but to the Ethereum network and miners.
My transaction timed out.

When a transaction “times out”, it doesn’t mean the transaction has failed, only that the Ethereum network is very busy. As a result, your bet won’t be displayed on Bether until the transaction is finalized in the network.

This can occur because of high gas prices or a spike in network congestion.
I didn’t receive ether for a winning or draw bet

After the match was finished, the result will be confirmed by the API and our Admin less than 24 hours from the time the match starts. Right after the result is finalized, your payouts will be sent out.
Tell me more about “gas”.

“Gas” is a shorthand used to describe the cost of powering a transaction or contract in Ethereum, which is the blockchain network that Bether is built on.

Because blockchain is decentralized, every transaction is distributed through multiple computers, not a central server. This ensures each bet is secure. It also takes a lot of computational power, which is covered by the cost of gas.

“Gas” is composed of two parts: Gas Price and Gas Limit. Gas Price is what you offer to pay the miners (in a tiny measurement of ether called “gwei”) for each operation to execute the smart contract. Gas Limit is how many operations you let them to do before they run out of gas and drop the transaction.

1 gwei = 1/1,000,000,000th of an ether.

You can get more information here https://ethgasstation.info

You can send the email to our support team support@bether.bet with the following information:

- Your Ethereum wallet address

- A description of your issue

- Transaction ID (can be found at the first column in “My bets”)

We aim to respond to support requests within 24 hours. We record the details of every bet into the blockchain, so we should always be able to resolve any issue that you may be experiencing.
