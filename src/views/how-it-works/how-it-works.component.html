How It Works

All matches will be managed by smart contracts thanks to the evolution of the Blockchain in general and the Ethereum in specific. A match goes through the following phases:

  • Open: The open opens 1 week before the match starts. You can bet on either team as often you want with as much Ether as you want. The minimum bet amount is 0.001 Ether, which is about 50 cents right now.
  • Closed/Match playing: The bet closes 15 mins before the match starts. It is too late to place bets now, as the match is starting soon.
  • Match finished: When the regular match tie is over (90’), the match result goes through 2 stages confirmation, one for the API result fetching and one from the Admin. The fetching and confirmation of the match outcome have to be completed within 1 day from after the match starts. If it takes longer, you can claim refunds.
  • Payouts: Once the winner is fetched and confirmed, there is no go-back. Users, who betted on the winning team will receive their payouts with 5% juice cut (We believe we are offering the lowest juice in comparison with other betting systems). In case there are no winning sides, refunds will be sent out to bettors with 2,5% juice cut each side.

Create a new bet

Step 1: Select an open match and choose the favorite team

- the first team will be selected by default

Step 2: Select the odds which were pre-defined by the Admin and the stake to bet.

- For example, the match between Russia and Australia, Russia is your favorite team and you choose the odds +1.

+ Win: In order for you to win your bet, Russia must keep the match draw or win with any goals.

+ Draw: If Australia wins 1 goal, the result with your handicap applied is a draw and your stake will be refunded with 2,5% juice cut.

+ Lose: Otherwise, you will lose.

- Let’s take another example with another odds, your favorite team is also Russia and you choose the odds -1.

+ Win: In order for you to win the bet, Russia must win the match at least 2 goals.

+ Draw: If Russia just wins 1 goal, the result with you handicap applied is a draw and your stake will be refunded with 2,5% juice cut.

+ Lose: Otherwise, you will lose.

- Next, bettors input the stake which is the amount of Ether for the bet. The minimum bet amount is 0.001 Ether, which is about 50 cents right now. There are no maximum ceilings for the bet amount, you can bet as much as you can afford for.

- Click Create button to proceed with the next step.

Step 3: Submit the transaction

- A MetaMask notification will be displayed to submit the transaction. The amount is the stake you have defined when setting up a new bet. The default gas price is set to 6 GWei. If you want to accelerate the transaction, you can do so by increasing the amount you pay per unit of gas, the excess gas will be refunded back to you after the transaction was finished.

- Click Submit button to submit the transaction

Final Step: Share with your friends or relatives

- After submitting the transaction, it might take a few moments for the transaction to be confirmed by the miners and then your bet will be displayed on our system with the status open. Your bet has to wait for other bettors to settle before it is changed to the status settled. You can share your bet via any social media to your friends or any relatives to make the settling process faster.

Settle a bet

Step 1: Select an open match

Step 2: Choose a bet you want to settle

- Upon access the match details, all bets will be displayed by default. You can filter the open bets by clicking Open (on the top left of the bets list), the number next to Open is the total open bets. The list of open bets will be filtered and now you just choose the bet with the odds and the stake you want, then click on Bet button

Step 3: Settle the bet

- Upon click on Settle, the bet details will be displayed. You can check the odds and the maximum stake which was offered by the bettor placing the bet. You can adjust the stake you want as long as it is less than the maximum amount offered and greater than the minimum amount required by our system.

- For example, the match between Russia and Australia, another bettor who placed the bet chose Russia with the odds +1. You choose this bet to settle, it means that you bet on Australia with the handicap -1

+ Win: In order for you to win the bet, Australia must win at least 2 goals

+ Draw: If Australia wins 1 goal, the result with you handicap applied is a draw and your stake will be refunded with 2,5% juice cut.

+ Lose: Otherwise, you will lose.

- Click on Settle button to settle the bet.

Step 4: Submit the transaction

- A MetaMask notification will be displayed to submit the transaction. The amount is the stake of your selected bet. The default gas price is set to 6 GWei. If you want to accelerate the transaction, you can do so by increasing the amount you pay per unit of gas, the excess gas will be refunded back to you after the transaction was finished.

- Click Submit button to submit the transaction

Final Step: Share with your friends or relatives

- After submitting the transaction, it might take a few minutes for the transaction to be confirmed by the miners and then your bet will be displayed on our system with settled status. Now just waiting for the match finished and get the payout if you are the winner.
