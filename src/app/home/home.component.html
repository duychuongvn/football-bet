Crypto P2P Betting

Bet Ether on your favorite teams (or draw result) for every match of the FIFA World Cup 2018 in Russia.

All matches will be managed by smart contacts deployed on the Ethereum blockchain. By calling its functions you can bet on either team competing in a match. After the match is decided, the payout will be sent out to the people who betted correctly.

Other than betting on the wrong team, there is no way of losing Ether. The design of the deployed Bet smart contracts makes this betting dApp (distributed application) fraud- and tamper-proof! Your bets are safely stored in the smart contracts. In case of worst scenarios (match is postponed, external data provider misbehaves, etc), you are always able to get all your Ether back without paying any fee!
